Research on Fermat's Last Theorem

My research focuses on geometric approaches to Fermat's Last Theorem, one of the most celebrated problems in number theory. The work explores triangle-based interpretations, geometric structures, and elementary methods for analysing higher-power Diophantine equations.

The objective is to investigate relationships between geometry and number theory, providing new perspectives on equations of the form xⁿ + yⁿ = zⁿ for n > 2.
